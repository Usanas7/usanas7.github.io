**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

A love story about two Nigerians who leave their country for the West. It explores race, identity, and belonging in a way that's both smart and funny.

Ifemelu is a fantastic character. Her observations about America are sharp and insightful.

This is a must-read.
