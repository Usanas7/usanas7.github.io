**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

A retelling of Greek mythology from the perspective of Circe, the witch who turns sailors into pigs.

This book gives voice to a character who was always seen as a villain. Her journey from outcast to powerful woman is inspiring. The writing is gorgeous.

Highly recommend for mythology fans.
