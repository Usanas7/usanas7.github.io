A blameless culture treats failures as learning opportunities rather than opportunities for punishment.

## Key Practices

- **Post-Incident Reviews:** Focus on systems, not people
- **Psychological Safety:** Team members can raise concerns without fear
- **Transparency:** Incidents are documented and shared
- **Continuous Improvement:** Each incident leads to system improvements

Organizations with blameless cultures experience higher innovation, better retention, and fewer repeated incidents.
