Policy as Code (PaC) is the practice of encoding compliance rules and security policies in machine-readable formats that can be automatically enforced.

## Benefits

- **Consistency:** Policies apply uniformly across all environments
- **Speed:** Automated checks replace manual reviews
- **Auditability:** Every policy violation is recorded
- **Version Control:** Policies evolve alongside infrastructure

Tools like Open Policy Agent (OPA), Chef InSpec, and CloudFormation Guard make policy as code accessible.
