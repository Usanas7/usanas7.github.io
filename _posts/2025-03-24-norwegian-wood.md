**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

This is a love story set in 1960s Tokyo. It's sad, nostalgic, and beautifully written.

The main character, Toru, is torn between two very different women. The book explores grief, loneliness, and first love.

It's slower than most books but worth the read.
