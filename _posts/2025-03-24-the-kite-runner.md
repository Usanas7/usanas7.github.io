**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

This book tells the story of Amir and Hassan, two boys growing up in Kabul. Their friendship, betrayal, and redemption is heartbreaking and beautiful.

The writing is powerful and emotional. The scene with the kite running is unforgettable. This book stayed with me long after I finished it.

If you haven't read this, I highly recommend it.
