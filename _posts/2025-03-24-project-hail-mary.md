**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

A man wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory. He must save humanity from extinction.

This book is smart, funny, and surprisingly heartwarming. The science is fascinating but easy to understand. The friendship that develops is one of my favorites in any book.

A must-read for sci-fi fans.
